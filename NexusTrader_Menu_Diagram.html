Section Map

  • DASHBOARDS — at-a-glance overview pages: Dashboard, Risk Management, AI Intelligence, Market Regime
  • TRADING — active execution tools: Market Scanner, Chart Workspace, Strategies, Paper Trading
  • RESEARCH & SIGNALS — pre-trade analysis: Backtesting, Signal Explorer, News & Sentiment
  • TRADING ANALYSIS — post-trade review: Orders & Positions, Performance Analytics
  • SYSTEM — infrastructure: Notifications, System Health, Exchange Management, Settings
  • (unlabelled) — Logs, Help Center — always last

What Changed vs v1

  • Market Regime moved to DASHBOARDS ← was INTELLIGENCE
  • Paper Trading moved to TRADING ← was RESEARCH

  • News & Sentiment moved to RESEARCH & SIGNALS ← was INTELLIGENCE
